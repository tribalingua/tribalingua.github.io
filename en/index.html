We understand that all students are different.

Our classes are designed based on many years of experience. We adapt the classes according to learner needs, speaking and listening activities are used in the classroom to encourage production and communication.

Our teaching processs

Interactive material

Interactive whiteboards, video links and boardgames to share and enjoy. Fun fun fun!

Realistic topics

Classes are designed to encourage speaking about interesting themes. Never a dull moment. Classes are updated every semester!

Focus on speaking

Tribalingua avoids boring grammar-based textbook classes. If you’re looking to improve your speaking skills and confidence, this is the place for you!

Small groups

Maximum six students per group. Makes learning faster and students have more opportunity to practise speaking. Fantastic!

Online Classes

All our classes are online. The tribalingua virtual school has classrooms and corridors, coffee machines and chill-out areas. It’s like being in a real school (without the boring classes!)

Gamefication

Playing and learning are natural partners. Our classes are game-based and always great fun.
