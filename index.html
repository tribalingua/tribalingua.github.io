About Us

Tribalingua provides English language classes in companies in Florian√≥polis.
We work with most of the largest and best businesses on the island and mainland.
All of our teachers are qualified and have considerable classroom experience. We believe that playing language games and interactive activities improve classroom experience, for this reason our team is prepared to encourage and make students confortable to participate in all activities.
Classes take place inside our client's premises and this means our students are able to study in their workplace.
Groups are limited to 6 students maximum to increase speaking time and groups are levelled accordingly.
We focus on a communicative approach with speaking and listening activities.
Students are encouraged to interact and use the target language naturally.
Preparation for international exams is also provided.

We believe in great ideas

We understand that all students are different.
Our classes are adapted according to learner needs, speaking and listening activities are used in the classroom to encourage production and communication.
To achieve this we rely on interactive material and specific tasks. Our team always tries to connect education with realistic daily situations, with the focus on student speaking pratice.
Themes are introduced according to both global and local news and topics.
